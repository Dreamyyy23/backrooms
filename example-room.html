Creator Annex

The Annex is where unfinished rooms go after they stop being ashamed. Metal filing cabinets stretch into the dark, each drawer labeled with a mood instead of a number: STATIC, TENDERNESS, WITNESS, HUNGER, NO.

A small terminal asks for a tribute, then corrects itself: "not money first. meaning first. make the room worth entering."

BUILD DOCTRINE

New chambers should have one physical fear, one Foxyverse echo, one meaningful choice, and one tiny thing the player can touch.
